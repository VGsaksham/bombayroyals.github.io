Product Description

This exquisite Silk Embroidered Blouse is a testament to the rich heritage of Indian craftsmanship. Meticulously handcrafted by skilled artisans, each piece features intricate embroidery that tells a story of tradition and elegance.

Made from 100% pure silk, this blouse offers a luxurious feel against the skin and a subtle sheen that catches the light beautifully. The embroidery is done using traditional techniques passed down through generations, ensuring each stitch is placed with precision and care.

The blouse features:

  • Intricate hand embroidery on the front panel and sleeves
  • Premium quality silk fabric
  • Comfortable fit with subtle darting for shape
  • Versatile design that pairs well with both traditional and contemporary bottoms
  • Ethically produced by local artisans

Perfect for formal occasions, festive celebrations, or whenever you want to make a statement with timeless elegance.

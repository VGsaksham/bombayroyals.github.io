Our Story

Crafting timeless elegance with a modern twist

The Bombay Royals Legacy

Founded in 2005 by designer Priya Sharma, Bombay Royals began as a small boutique in the heart of Mumbai's fashion district. What started as a passion project quickly evolved into one of India's most respected clothing brands, known for blending traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design.

Our journey is rooted in the rich textile heritage of India, drawing inspiration from the opulent royal courts of bygone eras while embracing modern aesthetics and sustainable practices.

Sustainability

Inclusivity

Innovation

Our Process

01

Design

Our creative team draws inspiration from India's rich cultural heritage, architectural marvels, and natural landscapes to create unique designs that tell a story.

02

Material Selection

We source the finest fabrics from ethical suppliers, prioritizing natural fibers like silk, cotton, and linen that are kind to both the skin and the environment.

03

Crafting

Our skilled artisans bring designs to life using traditional techniques such as hand embroidery, block printing, and hand-weaving, ensuring each piece is unique.

04

Quality Control

Every garment undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets our high standards before reaching our customers.
